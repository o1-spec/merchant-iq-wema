Create a high-fidelity clickable web application prototype called MerchantIQ.

MerchantIQ is an AI-powered Financial Intelligence and Business Health platform for African SMEs, traders, POS agents, restaurants, pharmacies, wholesalers, and small retail businesses.

The platform transforms transaction history into business insights, funding readiness assessments, and a verified financial identity that can be shared with lenders.

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Do NOT make this look like a generic AI product.

Do NOT use glowing AI gradients, futuristic effects, floating holograms, or sci-fi designs.

Design should feel like:

- Stripe Dashboard
- Mercury Banking
- Monzo Business
- Linear
- Notion

Keywords:
Professional
Trustworthy
Financial
Operational
Executive
Minimal
Premium

Color palette:
- White backgrounds
- Soft grays
- Dark navy accents
- Emerald success states
- Amber warning states
- Minimal use of purple

Typography:
- Inter
- Geist
- Plus Jakarta Sans

The product should feel like software used to run a business, not an AI experiment.
